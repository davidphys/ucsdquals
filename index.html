Physics Qualifying Exam Flash Cards

Flash card questions for the UCSD physics qualifying exams. Check the desired subjects and start answering questions.

About the quiz. All questions are intended to be "small". ie, you should have an outline of the derivation memorized, and possibly the answer memorized too. Some can be solved in your head. Some are really difficult to answer immediately, but the idea of putting them on flash cards is to drill them until the solution methods and/or answers are immediate. It's fine to pull out a piece of paper for the first few times you encounter a question though. Warning: there are bound to be mistakes in here somewhere!

You can find the project at https://github.com/davidphys/ucsdquals. Send suggestions or corrections to dkmoore at ucsd dot edu.
